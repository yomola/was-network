February 2026 – Case Study: Lois & Eunice

Spiritual Legacy, Generational Faith, and Godly Influence

2 Timothy 1:5 Deuteronomy 6:6–7 Psalm 127:3–5

This month emphasises leaving a spiritual legacy by nurturing generational faith and exercising godly influence, teaching God's Word and raising children as blessings following faithful examples.

February 1 – Generational Faith Begins at Home

Scripture: 2 Timothy 1:5

Context: Timothy's faith was nurtured by his grandmother Lois and his mother Eunice. Faith passed through generations is powerful, demonstrating that consistent example shapes spiritual heritage. Today, consider how your family or spiritual mentors influence your faith.

Application: Thank God for the spiritual legacy in your life. Pray for wisdom to pass faith to others.

February 2 – Teaching by Example

Scripture: Deuteronomy 6:6–7

Context: Faith is not just taught in words but lived daily. Lois and Eunice exemplified godly living, showing Timothy how to walk with God through their actions. Daily habits of obedience leave lasting impressions.

Application: Reflect on one way to live out your faith so others can learn from your example.

February 3 – Children as a Blessing

Scripture: Psalm 127:3–5

February 4 – Faith That Lasts

Scripture: 2 Timothy 3:14–15

Context: Timothy learned the Scriptures from childhood. A solid foundation in God's Word strengthens faith for a lifetime of ministry. Generational faith is cultivated through consistent teaching.

Application: Commit to daily Scripture reading or mentoring someone in God's Word this month.

February 5 – Living Faith Out Loud

Scripture: Proverbs 22:6
