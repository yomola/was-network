About Woman Arise and Shine Network

The Woman Arise and Shine Network (WAS Network) is a thriving ministry founded by Pastor Omotayo Alawode, established to inspire, instruct, and empower women to rise into their God-ordained identity and destiny.

Through this dynamic platform, we are raising a generation of spiritually grounded, purpose-driven women leaders who are transforming their homes, communities, and professional spheres with godly wisdom and excellence.

Vision

To see women step into their God-given purpose, reflecting His glory and brilliance in every area of life.

Equipped, empowered, and made whole, women will fulfill their divine calling in their homes, communities, and every sector where God has placed them.

Mission

WAS is committed to encouraging, counselling and equipping women to embrace and walk in their divine purpose by providing spiritual guidance, practical tools, and emotional support. Through teaching, mentorship and worship sessions, the aim is to bring healing to the spirit, soul, and mind so that each woman may arise and shine.

With the help of the Holy Spirit, using both offline and online platforms, WAS is dedicated to hosting transformative conferences that inspire, empower, and prepare women for their unique callings.

Our Programs

WAS Devotional

Focused devotional sessions studying the remarkable women in the Bible, drawing lessons from their faith, strength, and walk with God to inspire purposeful living today.

WAS Prayer Gatherings

Monthly prayer meetings where women intercede for families, nations, and the fulfillment of divine destinies.

Discovery Workshops

Interactive sessions helping women uncover their God-given gifts and develop them for maximum impact.

Marketplace Ministry

Equipping professional women to excel in their careers while maintaining spiritual vitality.

Annual WAS Conference

Annual Conference for refreshing, renewal, and deeper encounters with God.
