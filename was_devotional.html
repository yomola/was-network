January 2026 – Case Study: Esther

Divine Positioning, Courage, and Calling

Esther 1–10 Proverbs 3:5–6 Psalm 138:8

This month emphasises how God positions us for His purposes, orchestrates divine timing, and calls us to act with courage. Esther’s story teaches that no placement or moment is accidental. Trusting God fully, walking in integrity, and stepping boldly into His plan brings transformation, victory, and legacy.

Week 1

Identity, Preparation and Providence

Weekly Focus: Understanding our God-given identity, trusting His preparation process, and recognising His providence in our lives.

January 1 – Identity in God’s Providence

Scripture: Esther 1:1–9

Context: King Xerxes hosts a lavish feast to display human power, yet God is at work behind the scenes, positioning Esther for a divine purpose. Though the world focuses on authority and appearances, God carefully orchestrates events for His glory, even through ordinary circumstances.

Application: Ask God to reveal how He is positioning you in this season and pray for awareness and trust in His timing.
